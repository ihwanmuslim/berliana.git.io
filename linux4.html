How to Make Android as a PC / Laptop Webcam [Linux] - noahlinux.tech

  • September 2, 2020
  • In Tutorial Linux

Maybe a lot of people feel their laptop camera is not friendly / bad when used to record themselves. Like in live video conference sessions, video calls, or meetings using zoom and other needs.

Buying a webcam is not a suitable solution when it is not compatible with the contents of the wallet. This time I will share how to use Android as a tool / webcam. In fact, almost everyone has an Android with a good camera and certainly better than their original laptop camera / pc camera.

You need install Droidcam Software on your linux. You can get it in the AUR repo and for android you can get it in the playstore.
